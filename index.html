hello, I'm

byron abt

I earned my Bachelors of Science in TV and Film production from the University of SUNY Fredonia. From there I worked my way through the independent film production industry. I produced(and still produces)wedding videos, short films, commercials, and music videos. Through my work I was able to receive an opportunity at the University of Missouri as a part of their SEC Network Broadcast department. I started off as a Production Assistant and worked my way up the ladder to Lead Production Assistant, Camera Operator and finally Lead Camera Operator. I worked with the University of Missouri from Aug 2014-Sep 2017.

In August of 2015, I was recommended for a professional camera operating freelance position with ESPN through ABC. My work has taken me across states and to numerous sports; from gymnastics to baseball, softball, swimming, tennis, cross country, golf, track, basketball and football. However, my aspirations have always resided within becoming a Director and Producer for ESPN.
